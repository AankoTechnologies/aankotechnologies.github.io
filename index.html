Who We Are

Aanko Technologies is a California Certified Minority and Veteran-owned Small Business Enterprise providing safety and security solutions since the summer of 1997. We have offices in Northern California, Los Angeles California, Seattle Washington, Huston Texas, Baltimore Maryland, and Honolulu Hawaii.

We work with clients to analyze their Risk. Our assessments determine the where and why of investing valuable resources to protect their interests, before rolling out safety and security projects.

We are not aligned with any one product or methodology. We create solutions from best practices to promote your revenue generation and employee productivity, instead of selling a specific product that can din your funds and may not further your strategic objectives.
