About Aanko Technologies, Inc

Aanko Technologies is a California Certified Minority and Veteran-owned Small Business Enterprise providing safety and security solutions since 2001. We have offices in Northern California, Los Angeles County, and the Seattle, Washington Metropolitan Area.

We work with clients to analyze their Risk. Our assessments determine the where and why of investing valuable resources to protect their interests, before rolling out safety and security projects.

Executive Leadership

Steve Longoria, Chief Executive Officer

Steve is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Aanko.He has over 41 years of progressive and highly successful experience in assessing, designing and deploying safety, security and emergency management projects and systems in the public and private sectors. He has successfully project managed over 10,000 Safety, Security, Emergency Management and Continuity of Operations client projects in the past 20 years within all 16 national critical infrastructures.This includes leading development of the State of California Business Continuity Program, the State of California Energy Assurance Plan, the multi-western state energy assurance exercise, and the largest terrorism response exercise in State of California history. His expertise also extends to K-12 schools, institutes of higher education, and global resiliency partnerships in every continent. Steve is theonlyperson in Department of Defense history officially recognized as the top expert in three different career classifications: Antiterrorism, Security and Safety during a 21-year U.S. Air Force (USAF) military career.

Ricardo Cruz Gomez, Senior Vice President, Science and Technology

Ricardo is a versatile project manager with over 25 years of experience in various fields and is a former U.S. Navy Medical Service Corps Officer. He has been a professor of Project Management at Seattle Central College and Year Up, a nationwide technology training program. Ricardos project leadership spans both government and private sectors. He is an expert in emergency management, business continuity planning, tactical combat casualty care, and information technology. He also led the development and administration of DATAMINION, a SaaS artificial intelligence platform for workflow automation and document intelligence. Highlights of his career include: the California Energy Assurance Plan and California Statewide Business Continuity Program development, the California Department of Food and Agriculture Hoof and Mouth Disease and Avian Flu Response, Covered California Security Assessment and Business Continuity Planning, development and evaluation of full-scale terrorism exercises, as well as medical technology systems training of doctors and nurses.

Liz Jordan-Wettstein, Senior Vice President, Business Development

With over 18 years of experience in security technology and emergency management, Liz has designed, implemented, and managed solutions for commercial, government, and industrial sectors, specializing in surveillance, access control, intrusion detection, cybersecurity, and emergency operations. She combines strong technical expertise with extensive project management skills, having successfully led numerous high-profile projects from planning through delivery. Known for tailoring solutions to client needs, Liz ensures robust protection, effective crisis response, and seamless integration of technical systems with sound project management, consistently delivering high-quality, cost-effective results on time and within scope.

Dan Voreyer, Associate Vice President

Dan is a retired Fire Chief from the City of San Bruno, CA. He has over 40 years of demonstrated exceptional experience as a firefighter and emergency manager. Dan is a multi-talented, flexible professional with proven ability as a Field Training Officer. He has written comprehensive emergency management plans for city and county government. Dan is an articulate speaker and trainer and has led emergency teams as an Emergency Operations Center Manager. Dan Abrams, Sr. Director, Emergency Management

Dan is a nationally acclaimed retired Fire Battalion Chief and Paramedic from the City of Redwood City, CA. He has over 40 years in the fire service and has been the San Mateo County (CA) Fire Academy Director and a primary Instructor for Fire Science at the College of San Mateo. Dans accolades include a Presidential Citation for his response to Hurricane Katrina. He responded in the recovery mode to the 2009 San Bruno California Natural Gas Pipeline Explosion as a Captain on an engine company. Dan has written comprehensive emergency management plans and has trained public and private sector entities on National Incident Management System (NIMS) compliance. He is a certified Fire Technology instructor through the State of California and has been a member of FEMA USAR CATF-3 since the early 90s, serving as a Rescue Specialist and Plans Section Chief.

Steve Blanc, Sr. Director, Security Training
